Global settings

Username: Name that will be shown for you in you zoom meeting.

Auto accept user agreement: Whether or not to accept the user agreement automatically.

Default url prefix: The url prefix you mostly use, because of your company or university (i.e. "tu-berlin" for "tu-berlin.zoom.us"). This will be prepended to zoom urls, if not specified differently.

Block zoom client download: Whether or not to block the download of the zoom client.
